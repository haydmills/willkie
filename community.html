Get Involved in Our Community

Residents in Willkie have many opportunities to grow and develop. Check out some of the awesome opportunities available here at Willkie!

Willkie residents at a program hosted by the CMs

Floor Programs

Willkie Community Managers, Program Liaisons, Community Educators, and the Student Government all strive to host engaging and interactive programs throughout the year in Willkie. This is truly the best time to meet new friends living in Willkie. Don’t miss out on this great opportunity go out and actively participate in our community! You can keep up to date with floor and center wide events by following our Willkie social media accounts.



Willkie Little 500 Cycling Team

Willkie has had competitive men's and women's Little 500 teams over the years. Teams are formed on a year by year basis with the Willkie residents interested being the ones who organize the team and practice together. Willkie Residence Center helps to provide funding for our teams bikes, uniforms, gear, and training materials. If interested in learning more email: willkctr@indiana.edu

Willkie Student Government

Willkie Student Government seeks to enhance the residential experience by providing educational programming, resources, events, and an outlet for student advocacy. They are a part of the Residential Halls Association (RHA) which is made up of the student governments of each of IU's 13 residence centers, plus a campus-level group of executives, directors, a programming board, and a conduct board. In total, RHA has around 300 student leaders, making it one of the largest student organizations at IU.



Willkie Conduct Board

Willkie Conduct Board fosters student learning and development by holding student organizations accountable to the IU Code of Student Rights, Responsibilities, and Conduct. The collaborative conduct board process enhances student civility, character, and citizenship while building a sense of community, responsibility, and a respect for diversity.

Volunteer Opportunities

Every year Willkie strives to make connections with Bloomington non-profit organizations in order to plan volunteer opportunities for the Willkie community to give back. Past year volunteering has been the Interfaith Winter Shelter and the local food bank.



Work at Willkie

There are many employment opportunities at Willkie. You can apply to work the Willkie Center Desk, work at the Willkie C-Store, or work at the Willkie library. Email willkctr@indiana for more information.
