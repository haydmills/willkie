Housing Options

Willkie Residence Center @ Indiana University Bloomington

Find Your Home on Campus

Willkie Residence Center offers many different housing options for a great on campus price. Whether you are looking to live alone or with a group of friends Willkie has options for you. Here below are the different housing options available.

Housing Options

Small Single

Our small single rooms in Willkie offer a closet, desk with a chair, nightstand, single bed, and a clothing drawer. The small single room is connected to a shared bathroom with another small single room on the other side.

Standard Single

The standard single rooms in Willkie offer a closet, desk with a chair, nightstand, single bed, and a clothing drawer. The standard single room is connected to a shared bathroom with another standard single room on the other side.

Take a video tour inside

Large Single

The large single rooms in Willkie offer a spacious layout which includes a closet, desk with a chair, nightstand, single bed, and a clothing drawer. The large single room is connected to a shared bathroom with another room on the other side.

Take a video tour inside



Two Bedroom Suite

The two bedroom suite in Willkie offers apartment style living on campus. They include two private bedrooms, one shared private bathroom, one living room area. Each private bedroom includes a closet, desk with a chair, nightstand, single bed, and a clothing drawer. The bathroom includes two sinks with a walk in shower. The living room area includes a couch, floor lamp, and a coffee table.

Three Bedroom Suite

The three bedroom suite in Willkie offers apartment style living on campus. They include three private bedrooms, one shared private bathroom, one living room area. Each private bedroom includes a closet, desk with a chair, nightstand, single bed, and a clothing drawer. The bathroom includes two sinks with a walk in shower. The living room area includes a couch, floor lamp, and a coffee table.

Take a video tour inside

Mason Rooms

Mason rooms offer three to four bedroom apartment style living arrangements. Which include a bathroom with a walk in shower and two sinks. Each private bedroom includes a closet, desk with a chair, nightstand, single bed, and a clothing drawer. The living room area includes a couch and a coffee table.

Willkie Buildings

North Tower

Houses nine floors of residential living. The ground floor has an entertainment room, laundry washers and dryers, printing stations, music practice room, and is where the Willkie center desk is located where residents are able to get pick up their mail and packages.

Center Building

Contains the Willkie library, C-Store, computer room, printing/scanning stations, auditorium, music practice rooms as well as two towers with four floors of residential apartment style living.

South Tower

Houses nine floors of residential living. The ground floor has an entertainment room, laundry washers and dryers, printing stations, music practice room, and a workout room with elliptical machines, treadmills, yoga mats, and yoga balls.

Mason Apartments

Home to four floors of residential apartment style living. Also has an entertainment room, laundry washers and dryers, and printing stations available.
