We are Willkie.

Willkie Residence Center @ Indiana University Bloomington



Welcome to Willkie Residence Center at Indiana University a diverse upperclassmen community conveniently located in the southwest neighborhood on the Bloomington campus.





Your New Home

Willkie Residence Center offers many different housing options for a great on campus price. Whether you are looking to live alone or with a group of friends, Willkie has options for you.



Learn More

Diverse Community

Residents from all across the world with diverse cultural backgrounds live in Willkie. Willkie has many different living communities and groups to get involved with for whatever you're looking for.



Learn More

Great Amenities

Music practice rooms, basketball, tennis, and volleyball courts, floor kitchens, floor lounges, a workout room, game rooms, a library, an auditorium, and a C-Store are all located in Willkie Residence Center.
