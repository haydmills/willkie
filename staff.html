Meet our Team at Willkie

Willkie Residence Center would not run so smoothly if it weren’t for all of our staff members. Here below you can meet many of the staff that work at Willkie.

Willkie Professional Staff

Our professional staff are the ones who are responsible for the daily management of Willkie Residence Center. They are responsible for supervising the rest of the Willkie staff and direct the business operations. They seek to promote community responsibility by maintaining ongoing contact with residents to deal with behavioral problems and to stay informed of residents’ views, needs, and issues. They also serve as liaisons between the residents and the RPS administrative staff.

Willkie Community Managers and Educator

Our community managers live and work in Willkie. They serve as a resource to Willkie residents as well as enforce university and residence hall policies and regulations in order to help maintain an environment conducive to learning. They plan programs for their floors in order to help challenge residents. We also have a Community Educator in Willkie who is committed to working on diversity and social justice awareness and education.

Willkie Secretaries

The Willkie Secretaries perform routine clerical and administrative duties. They organize files, draft messages, schedule appointments, and support other Willkie Residence Center staff.

Willkie Environmental Operations Staff

Environmental Operations keeps all buildings in Willkie Residence Center in excellent working order. Each floor and building in Willkie has specific Environmental Operations (EO) staff members who are responsible for maintenance and upkeep. Their goal is to provide a clean, comfortable, well-maintained environment for all Willkie residents.
